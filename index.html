Short Bio

I am pursuing my Masters in Space Science & Technology at Universit√§t Bremen, Germany. I am especially interested in the exploration of the interplay between intelligence and networks in distributed intelligence, especially for the development of future space systems.

Currently, I am pursuing my interest in networked intelligent space systems along with my coursework: I am developing a swarming satellite simulator based on Alfonso Gonzalez's AWP repository to create orbital swarming scenarios to test further usecases. I am also independently studying the research work being published by Universitat Bremen's ANT Research Group on federated learning for satellite constellations and applications of swarming satellites. I am a beginner contributor to the LibreCube Open Source Project, working on an open source implementation for the Space Data Link Protocol. I am also trying to learn more about Artificial Intelligence, Optical Communications and Quantum Networks in my spare time.

I hold a Bachelors of Technology in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Sardar Vallabhbhai National Institute of Technology . The topic of my Bachelors thesis was Deep Learning for Automatic Modulation Recognition of Unidentified Signals. Our work was heavily influenced by the Timothy O'Shea's and . Since 2021, I've worked as a communications engineer at Larsen & Toubro Technology Services's 5G Networks Lab and NewSpace Research & Technology, working on O-RAN 5G System Integration for private networks, specially focussing on timing and synchronization distribution networks, and development of network centric software solutions, primarily for swarm UAVS operated by the Indian military.
